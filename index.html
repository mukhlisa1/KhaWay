What will we do in the free lesson?

Icebreaker Activity

We'll start with a fun icebreaker to get to know each other. You’ll introduce yourself and share a bit about your interests. This will help us create a comfortable learning environment.

Assessing Your English Level

Next, we’ll conduct a quick assessment to understand your current English level. This will include some basic questions and exercises, focusing on speaking, listening, reading, and writing skills. Don’t worry; it’s all about helping you find the right path!

Goal Setting

Based on your assessment, we’ll discuss your learning goals. Whether you want to improve your conversational skills, prepare for exams, or enhance your vocabulary, we'll tailor the course to meet your specific needs.

Our courses

General English

Description: This course is designed for learners at all levels who want to build a strong foundation in the English language. It covers essential grammar, vocabulary, reading, writing, listening, and speaking skills.
Duration: 8-12 weeks.
Target Audience: Beginners to advanced learners.
Key Topics:

  • Everyday vocabulary and expressions
  • Basic to advanced grammar structures
  • Reading comprehension and analysis
  • Writing skills, including essays and reports
  • Listening practice with diverse accents

Conversational English

Description: This course focuses on improving speaking and listening skills through interactive activities and real-life scenarios. It’s ideal for those looking to gain confidence in everyday conversations.
Duration: 6-8 weeks.
Target Audience: Intermediate learners and above.
Key Topics:

  • Engaging in small talk and discussions
  • Role-playing common social situations
  • Practicing pronunciation and intonation
  • Building vocabulary for various topics
  • Understanding idiomatic expressions

Business English

Description: Tailored for professionals, this course enhances English communication skills in a business context. It covers essential vocabulary and practices for meetings, presentations, negotiations, and networking.
Duration: 8-10 weeks.
Target Audience: Business professionals and students pursuing business careers
Key Topics:

  • Business terminology and jargon
  • Writing professional emails and reports
  • Conducting meetings and presentations
  • Negotiation techniques in English
  • Networking skills and cultural nuances

Frequently asked questions

Online courses typically involve a mix of video lessons, live sessions, readings, assignments, and quizzes. You can access materials through the course platform and interact with instructors and classmates in discussion forums or live sessions.

Many courses are self-paced, meaning you can complete lessons and assignments on your schedule. However, some courses may have live sessions or deadlines to keep everyone progressing together.

Access varies by course. Some provide lifetime access, while others limit access to the course duration or a set period after completion.

Typically, you’ll need a computer, internet access, and possibly a webcam and microphone for live sessions. Specific software requirements will be listed in the course details.

Refund policies vary by course. Some offer refunds within a certain period, while others may have no-refund policies. Please review the refund policy before enrolling..
