About us

At Khaway, we believe that learning English should be more than just a classroom experience—it should be a transformative journey. Founded with the vision of making English accessible to learners worldwide, Khaway is more than just an online school; it’s a community where students unlock their potential, build confidence, and connect with a world of opportunities.
Khaway was born from a simple idea: to make English learning engaging, effective, and convenient for people of all ages and backgrounds. Our dedicated team of expert teachers, curriculum designers, and support staff work together to create a learning experience that feels personal, purposeful, and fun.

What makes us unique?

Tailored Learning Paths

At Khaway, no two students are alike. That’s why we offer personalized learning plans that adapt to your level, pace, and goals. Whether you’re preparing for an exam, improving your conversational skills, or learning English for work, we have the tools and expertise to guide you.

Live, Interactive Classes

Forget boring, pre-recorded lessons. Our live online classes put you face-to-face with passionate, experienced teachers who make learning dynamic and interactive. You’ll practice real-world English with peers and receive immediate feedback to refine your skills.

Cultural Immersion

English is more than just a language—it’s a gateway to understanding cultures, building relationships, and exploring the world. Our courses integrate cultural lessons, real-life scenarios, and global perspectives, helping you learn not just how to speak English but how to live it.

Flexible Scheduling

Life is busy, and we get that. That’s why Khaway offers classes at all hours, so you can learn whenever and wherever it suits you. All you need is an internet connection, and you’re ready to start.
